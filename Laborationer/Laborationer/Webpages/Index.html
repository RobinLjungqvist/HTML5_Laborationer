General Information

About guitar

The guitar is an musical string instrument consisting of a the guitar body, neck, head and strings. Most commonly the guitar has 6 string, but it can vary. Some guitar virtuosos use up to ten or eleven strings. There are several different kinds of guitars the most common ones are acoustic or electrical.

Different styles

When performing on a guitar the different parts can be divided by rythm, lead or solo. When you play the guitar as a lonely instrument, solo guitar, it's usually performed on a classical acoustic guitar. The steelstring, commonly called folk, and electric guitar are more commonly played with a constallation of other instrument. Folk guitars are typically used for playing rythm rythm guitar while electrical guitars are used to play leads Lead guitar. Playing lead usually are connected to grandiose guitar solos in rock and metal songs, but the real meaning is that you play melodies rather than chords.

Is guitar hard?

Instruments in general are commonly thought to be very hard to master. Which might be true for some. But everyone can learn to play an instrument. The guitar require alot more coordination of the fingers in the beginning that some other instruments, but all that it takes is alot of practice and dedication.
