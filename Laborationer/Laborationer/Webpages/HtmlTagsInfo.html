Header

Keyword header within opening and closing tabs <>. The header is meant to contain a logo, or a headline for the website. It also usually contains navigation.

Navigation

Keyword nav within opening and closing tabs <>. The navigation is a list of link to the other pages of the website.

Section

Keyword section within opening and closing tabs <>. The section is used to grouping distinct content or functionality.

Article

Keyword article within opening and closing tabs <>. Is related to section but articles contains related content that can standalone without the rest of the website.

Aside

Keyword aside within opening and closing tabs <>. Aside is supposed to contain limited information related to an article but should be the article. Commonly used for Ads and related content.

Footer

Keyword footer within opening and closing tabs <>. Footer is connected to an article or the whole website and should contain contact information who wrote the website and legal notation such as copyright.
